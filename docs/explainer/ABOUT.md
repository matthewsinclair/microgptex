# About These Assets

The files in this directory were generated using [NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google.com/), prompted with the MicroGPTEx blog posts, README, and source code.

They are not authoritative, and they are not intended for publication or distribution.

What they are is a demonstration of what an LLM and generative AI can produce when given well-structured source material. They also serve as a useful tl;dr — a quick way to get the gist of the project without reading the full blog series or source code.

That said: even though these assets look and sound good — maybe even _great_ — they are not without error. They are not yet at the stage where you would want to rely on them as a primary reference. It's only a matter of time, though, before generative outputs like these go from merely looking and sounding good to being genuinely reliable and reusable.
